About E-By Design

At E-By Design, we take our curiosities seriously. We're a product design group specializing in custom software solutions with exceptional architecture and simple designs. Formed in 2002 and incorporated in 2009, we’re comprised of veteran product managers, designers, and developers skilled in an array of classic programming languages along side certifications in some of the newest technologies.

Our team builds original IP software solutions including SurgeROI, Entegrity, and Advantage. We also support customization of our Advantage software solution for clients such as Uber, Ontario Provincial Police, and Bell Canada on behalf of Triton Canada Inc.
